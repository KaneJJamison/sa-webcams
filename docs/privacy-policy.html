Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how NSW Coastal Webcams ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers. There are no user accounts, no registration, and no sign-in required.

The only data stored by the App is your list of favourite cameras, which is saved locally on your device using your device's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

a) CoastalCOMS Webcam & Weather Widgets

Live webcam feeds and weather data are provided by CoastalCOMS. When you view a camera or weather feed, your device connects directly to CoastalCOMS servers. Their service may log standard server information such as IP addresses. Please refer to coastalcoms.com for their privacy policy.

b) Google AdMob (Advertising)

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to show personalised or non-personalised ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. advertising ID)
  • General location information
  • Device and usage information

You can opt out of personalised advertising in your device settings: Settings → Privacy → Tracking (iOS). For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and Google AdMob policies.

c) OpenStreetMap (Map View)

The Explore map view uses map tiles from OpenStreetMap. Tile requests are made directly from your device. See the OpenStreetMap Privacy Policy for details.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information
  • We do not collect precise location data
  • We do not share any data with third parties beyond the services listed above
  • We do not sell any data

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
